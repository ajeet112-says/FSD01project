What is AI?

 AI is a field of computer science that aims to create systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include:
Understanding language (Natural Language Processing)
Recognizing patterns and images (Computer Vision)
Making decisions (Machine Learning & Expert Systems)
Moving and interacting with the environment (Robotics) 

Types of AI
 
Narrow AI (Weak AI)
Designed for a specific task
Examples: Siri, Google Translate, ChatGPT
General AI (Strong AI)
Hypothetical AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can
Still under research and development
Superintelligent AI
Theoretical future AI that surpasses human intelligence in all aspects
Raises ethical and safety concerns
